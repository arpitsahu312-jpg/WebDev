Pineapple Cake

Black Forest Cake

Pineapple Cake is a light and refreshing sponge cake layered with whipped cream and juicy pineapple pieces. It is often decorated with pineapple slices, cherries, and cream swirls, making it a popular choice for birthdays and celebrations. Its sweet-tangy flavor and soft texture make it a favorite among all age groups.

 Black Forest Cake is a classic chocolate sponge cake layered with fresh whipped cream and cherries. The exterior is covered with chocolate shavings, creating a rich and elegant appearance. Its combination of moist chocolate, creamy frosting, and fruity cherries offers a perfectly balanced taste.

Red Velvet Cake

Cassata Cake

Red Velvet Cake is known for its vibrant red color,soft velvety crumb,and subtle cocoa flavor. It is traditionally layered with smooth cream cheese frosting, creating a delightful contrast between the rich cake and tangy icing. The elegant appearance makes it a popular choice for weddings, anniversaries, and special occasions.

Cassata Cake is a colorful celebration cake inspired by the classic Italian dessert and widely enjoyed in India. It features layers of soft sponge cake filled with whipped cream, mixed fruits, nuts, and candied fruit (tutti frutti). Often decorated with vibrant cream, cherries, and fruit toppings, it offers a refreshing, creamy, and fruity taste.
